  <strong> Privacy Policy for *Scenario: Unknown* </strong>

<b>Effective Date:</b> 5/1/26
<b>Last Updated:</b> 5/1/26

<strong> 1. Overview </strong>

This Privacy Policy describes how *Scenario: Unknown* (“the Game,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles user information.

At this time, *Scenario: Unknown* is designed to operate entirely offline and does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data** from users.

<strong> 2. Information We Do Not Collect </strong>

We do <b>not</b> collect any of the following:

* Personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number)
* Device identifiers or IP addresses
* Location data
* Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
* Account or login information

The Game does not require user registration or account creation.

<strong> 3. Local Data Storage </strong>

All data generated by the Game is stored **locally on your device only**. This may include:

* Game progress
* Settings and preferences
* Saved scenarios or states

This data:

* Is not transmitted to external servers
* Is not accessible by us or any third party
* Can be deleted by uninstalling the Game or clearing app data through your device settings

<strong> 4. Third-Party Services </strong>

*Scenario: Unknown* does **not integrate any third-party services** that collect or process user data, including but not limited to:

* Analytics platforms
* Advertising networks
* Cloud storage providers

<strong> 5. Data Sharing </strong>

Because no data is collected, we do **not share any user data** with third parties.

<strong> 6. Children’s Privacy </strong>

<b>Scenario: Unknown</b> is not directed toward children under the age of 13. However, since no personal data is collected, there is no intentional or unintentional collection of children’s personal information.

If future updates introduce data collection, appropriate safeguards will be implemented to comply with applicable child privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

<strong> 7. Data Security </strong>

As the Game does not collect or transmit user data, there are no external data storage or transmission risks associated with its current version.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

<strong> 8. Future Features and Policy Updates </strong>

We may introduce new features in the future, such as:

* Online multiplayer or collaboration
* Cloud saves or account systems
* Leaderboards or social features

These features may require the collection and processing of limited user data.

If such changes occur:

* This Privacy Policy will be updated prior to implementation
* Users will be notified of material changes where required
* Any data collection will be clearly disclosed and require appropriate consent where applicable

<strong> 9. User Rights </strong>

Since we do not collect or process personal data, user rights related to data access, correction, deletion, or portability are not applicable at this time.

If future updates introduce data collection, users will be provided with appropriate rights in accordance with applicable laws.

<strong> 10. Compliance with Platform Requirements </strong>

This Privacy Policy is provided to comply with applicable platform policies, including distribution platforms such as Google Play.

Even though no user data is currently collected, this policy ensures transparency regarding the Game’s data practices.

<strong> 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy </strong>

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above.

Continued use of the Game after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
