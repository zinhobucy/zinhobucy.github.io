<h2>1. Overview</h2>
<p>
This Privacy Policy describes how <em>Scenario: Unknown</em> (“the Game,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles user information.
</p>
<p>
At this time, <em>Scenario: Unknown</em> is designed to operate entirely offline and does <strong>not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data</strong> from users.
</p>


<h2>2. Information We Do Not Collect</h2>
<p>We do <strong>not</strong> collect any of the following:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number)</li>
    <li>Device identifiers or IP addresses</li>
    <li>Location data</li>
    <li>Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data</li>
    <li>Account or login information</li>
</ul>
<p>The Game does not require user registration or account creation.</p>


<h2>3. Local Data Storage</h2>
<p>All data generated by the Game is stored <strong>locally on your device only</strong>. This may include:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Game progress</li>
    <li>Settings and preferences</li>
    <li>Saved scenarios or states</li>
</ul>
<p>This data:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Is not transmitted to external servers</li>
    <li>Is not accessible by us or any third party</li>
    <li>Can be deleted by uninstalling the Game or clearing app data through your device settings</li>
</ul>


<h2>4. Third-Party Services</h2>
<p>
<em>Scenario: Unknown</em> does <strong>not integrate any third-party services</strong> that collect or process user data, including but not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
    <li>Analytics platforms</li>
    <li>Advertising networks</li>
    <li>Cloud storage providers</li>
</ul>


<h2>5. Data Sharing</h2>
<p>
Because no data is collected, we do <strong>not share any user data</strong> with third parties.
</p>
